# Originality Statement

The Supply Chain Attestation standard, tools, and documentation are the original work of Korext.

While this project is designed to interoperate with existing software supply chain standards (such as CycloneDX and SPDX), it does not directly copy or reproduce the text of those specifications.

We acknowledge the incredible work done by the creators of CycloneDX 1.6, SPDX 2.3, Sigstore, and SLSA frames. Our tools act as complementary extensions within those namespaces to bring AI provenance to the software supply chain without duplicating prior effort.
